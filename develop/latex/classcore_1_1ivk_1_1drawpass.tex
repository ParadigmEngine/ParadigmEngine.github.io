\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A pass is a collection of drawcalls, grouped into sets of drawgroups, and a target framebuffer or swapchain. This describes a full pipeline (synced) to get something into a set of rendertargets and to either use it in subsequent passes, or present it to a core\+::gfx\+::surface. Passes also make sure that they don\textquotesingle{}t create race conditions with other passes that have been assigned as its dependencies.
